How to Use the AI Personality Feedback Analyzer

This AI-based tool helps you analyze and improve your **eye contact, posture, confidence, and speaking skills**. Follow the steps below to get started.

Step 1: **Enable Camera Access** Click **"Start Camera"** and grant access to your webcam.
Step 2: **Position Yourself Correctly** Sit in a **well-lit environment** and position your face **within the camera frame**.
Step 3: **Start Recording** Click **"Start Recording"** to begin the AI-powered analysis.
Step 4:** Maintain Natural Eye Contact** Look at the camera as if making **eye contact with a person**.
Step 5: **Speak Clearly and Confidently** Use a **steady tone, moderate speed, and clear pronunciation**.
Step 6: **Check Real-Time Feedback** Observe live AI analysis of your posture, confidence, and speaking style.
Step 7: **Stop and Save Recording** Click **"Stop Recording"** and **download the session** for review.
Step 8: **Analyze Results & Improve** Review feedback and improve your **body language, voice, and confidence**.

Troubleshooting & FAQs

  • Camera Not Working? - Ensure your browser has camera permissions enabled.
  • No Audio Feedback? - Check your microphone settings and ensure it's not muted.
  • AI Not Detecting Posture? - Ensure your **full upper body** is visible in the frame.
